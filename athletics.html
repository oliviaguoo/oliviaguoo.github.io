Athletics

Playing sports and staying active is such a huge part of my life. 8 years & counting of competitive volleyball, 9 years & counting of hiking/backpacking, 2 years & counting of distance running, here's a little summary of it all :D

Volleyball

Quick rundown: played all four years of high school where I was a pin hitter & the captain of the varsity team. Won team awards 4/4 years (2x most improved, 2x MVP), named First Team All-League, and school-wide student-athlete of the year (highest combined academic/athletic performance). I also played club volleyball for four years, started at Moxie Juniors Volleyball Club, then Northwest Juniors Volleyball Club, and ended at Sudden Impact Volleyball Club. Currently, I'm an outside hitter for Cal Women's Club Volleyball Team and the Chinese Bears NACIVT 9-man team. Since moving to the bay area, I've been introduced to many more play styles, namely beach, grass, and reverse coed volleyball, it's surprising how small changes in rules & the playing surface vastly alters a game with the same mechanics!!
